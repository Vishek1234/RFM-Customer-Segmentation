# 📊 RFM Customer Segmentation Project

## 🧠 Project Overview
This project focuses on customer segmentation using RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) analysis. The goal is to understand customer purchasing behavior and divide them into meaningful segments for better business decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Segment customers based on their buying behavior  
- Identify high-value customers  
- Detect at-risk and lost customers  
- Improve customer retention and marketing strategy  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
- Python  
- Pandas  
- NumPy  
- Matplotlib / Seaborn  
- Jupyter Notebook  

---

## 📊 Methodology
1. Data Cleaning and preprocessing  
2. RFM feature creation:
   - Recency (how recently customer purchased)
   - Frequency (how often customer purchases)
   - Monetary (how much customer spends)
3. Customer segmentation based on RFM scores  
4. Data visualization using graphs  
5. Insights generation  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- A small group of customers contributes major revenue (Champions)  
- Many customers show low frequency and need engagement  
- Some customers are at risk due to inactivity  
- New customers need onboarding strategies  

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Loyalty programs for Champions  
- Re-engagement campaigns for At-Risk customers  
- Upselling and cross-selling for high-value users  
- Discounts for new customers to improve retention
